The Basics

Hackverse at a Glance

Hackverse is a 30-hour open hackathon that will be organised by the IEEE Power and Energy Society chapter of Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology, Kochi.

What You Can Expect

Hackers from all over Kerala shall compete for prizes beyond your comprehension! Through gruelling sessions of continuous evaluation and a final presentation of your project, stand a chance to win cash prizes, services from top end companies, T-shirts, stickers, and a whole lot more!

When and Where

Due to the unprecedented impact of COVID-19, the event has been postponed from March to the month of August.

Our Prizes

Matic Awards

Rs. 15,000 for best project done using Matic Technologies.

Ethereum Awards

Rs. 10,000 for best project done using Ethereum Technologies.

First Prize

A cash prize of Rs. 5000 offered by RSET IEEE PES SBC.

Second Prize

A cash prize of Rs. 3000 offered by RSET IEEE PES SBC.

Wolfram Awards

Wolfram One services worth $379 for participants in the top 3 teams.

Maker Gram Awards

A Special Maker Gram sponsored Hardware Kit for team making the Best Hardware Project.

Bugsee Award

Bugsee credits worth $150 per head for First place and $100 for second place.

Pololu Hardware Kit

A Pololu Hardware kit for participants winning First place.
